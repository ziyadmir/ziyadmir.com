The Voice Test: AI Models Attempt My Writing Style

A Series on AI Mimicry • Started July 2025

About this experiment: What happens when you feed an AI model your own writing and ask it to mimic your voice? This series explores AI voice mimicry by having multiple models (Claude 4 Opus, Gemini 2.5 Pro, OpenAI o3, OpenAI o3 Pro, OpenAI Codex) write the same blog posts in my style.

I provided each model with a 3,000-word unpublished personal essay as a sample of my writing style. The goal? To see how well different AI models can capture individual writing voice, what the output looks like, and how readers perceive the different versions. It's an experiment in AI authenticity and the boundaries of voice replication.
