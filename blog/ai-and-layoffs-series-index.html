Series Overview

As AI capabilities advance at breakneck speed, the nature of work is fundamentally changing. This series explores the intersection of AI advancement and workforce dynamics, offering perspectives on how professionals can navigate an increasingly automated world.

Drawing from firsthand experience in both building AI systems and observing their impact on organizations, these essays examine the evolving relationship between human expertise and machine capability.

Understanding the new dynamics of value in an AI-augmented workplace

Why the traditional career ladder is becoming a capability gradient, and what this means for professional development in the age of AI. Explores how AI changes the economics of expertise and the new skills that matter.

Part 2: Senior ICs Are the New Managers

The rise of the orchestrator role in AI-powered organizations

As AI handles more routine coordination, senior individual contributors are evolving into a new kind of leader—one who orchestrates both human and AI capabilities to deliver outsized impact.
