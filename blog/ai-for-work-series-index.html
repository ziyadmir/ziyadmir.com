Series Overview

After leading AI deployment initiatives at scale, I've learned that enterprise AI adoption isn't about the models—it's about the infrastructure, governance, and cultural transformation required to make AI a productive part of daily engineering work.

This series shares battle-tested patterns for deploying AI tools in large engineering organizations, drawn from real-world experience deploying AI IDEs to thousands of developers and building agent orchestration platforms.

Posts in This Series

Part 1: Get Everyone an AI IDE - The Foundation of Enterprise AI

The first and most critical step in enterprise AI adoption

Before diving into complex agent systems, start with the basics: get every developer a properly configured AI IDE. This post covers deployment strategies, security considerations, and how to drive adoption at scale.

Part 2: Building a Centralized MCP Gateway - The Context Layer

How to securely connect AI tools to your internal systems

Model Context Protocol (MCP) is the bridge between AI and your enterprise data. Learn how to build a centralized gateway with proper authentication, audit logging, and access controls.

Part 3: From Proxy Logs to Intelligence - Enterprise AI Observability

Turn your security proxy into an AI analytics powerhouse

Your security proxy already sees every LLM request. This post shows how to build real-time analysis pipelines that provide both security monitoring and productivity insights.

Part 4: Agent Orchestration at Scale - Building an Enterprise Platform

From simple tools to complex multi-agent workflows

Learn how to build an enterprise agent orchestration platform that can handle everything from simple automations to complex multi-step workflows with proper governance.

Part 5: Preserving Institutional Knowledge in the Age of AI

Don't let your AI forget what your humans learned

Short retention policies and ephemeral communication channels are erasing institutional knowledge. This post presents a systematic approach to selective context preservation.

Part 6: Work-as-Code - The Micro-MCP Revolution

Making every engineering task programmatically accessible

The final frontier: turning every manual task into a callable function. Learn how to build a micro-services approach to MCP that enables true AI autonomy.

Part 7: Building AI Observability and Adoption Programs

Dashboards, metrics, and scaling AI adoption across your organization

Beyond deployment: how to measure AI impact, identify power users, and create systems that turn individual discoveries into organizational capabilities.
