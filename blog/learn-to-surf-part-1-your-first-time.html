Part 1: Your First Time - Getting Comfortable in the Water

August 1, 2025 · Learn to Surf Series

Surfing Beginner Tutorial Ocean

Learn to Surf Series - a practical guide to catching your first waves.

Your surfboard is your ticket to the ocean. Maybe you've lived near the beach for years but rarely ventured in—it always felt intimidating. Now, with a board and a wetsuit, you finally step into the water.

The shock is immediate: the water is cold and every wave shoves you backward. You try to paddle out, only to find yourself exhausted fifteen feet from shore.

The ocean is vast and powerful. Respect it and take your time.

Water fills your eyes and ears. You swallow a bit and wonder if buying the gear was a mistake. It's normal to feel overwhelmed during these first sessions.

Forget about riding waves for now. Stage one is simply getting comfortable being tossed around. Feel the chill, the pull of the tide, and the fatigue in your arms.

Keep paddling out and letting the surf push you back. Laugh when you stumble and focus on how the water feels. Once you can relax in the chaos, you're ready for the next step.
