Riding the Tech Wave: Lessons from Surfing

July 2, 2025

AI Learning Surfing

Surfing is a useful metaphor for technology shifts because the wave is not created by you. You can ignore it, paddle too late, panic when it lifts you, or learn how to position yourself before the energy arrives.

The core idea

The AI wave rewards early positioning. That does not mean blindly chasing every tool. It means getting enough reps that your body of judgment updates before the market has fully explained what happened.

Why it matters

People often wait for certainty before acting. By the time certainty is available, the best learning window is gone. The point is to experiment early enough that you understand the shape of the wave while others are still debating whether it is real.

How to use it

  • Paddle early by using the tools before the organizational mandate arrives.
  • Stay balanced by pairing excitement with verification and taste.
  • Keep scanning; each wave changes the local conditions for the next one.

Bottom line

The goal is not to predict every break perfectly. It is to build the reflexes to catch the important waves when they show up.
