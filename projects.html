Work

Selected work, with the public parts separated from the private parts.

This is not a complete resume. It is a small index of real projects, companies, and systems I can describe at a useful level without pretending every internal detail belongs on the open web.

One thread runs through most of it: teaching platforms to understand content well enough to act on it. Video moments at YouTube — the line of work that later launched publicly as Peak Points. Adversarial ad creative and interactive experiences at Roblox. Each generation of content is harder to read, the decisions riding on the read are more autonomous, and the open problems get steadily more research-shaped: evaluation, judgment, and what an AI system should be allowed to decide.

Current
