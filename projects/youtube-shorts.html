Project Overview

YouTube Select for Shorts was a strategic initiative I led to extend YouTube's premium advertising offering to the rapidly growing short-form video format. This project aimed to compete directly with TikTok Pulse and Instagram's contextual advertising offerings while leveraging YouTube's unique strengths in content understanding and brand safety.

The Challenge

Short-form video presented unique challenges for brand advertising:

  • Extremely brief viewing durations (15-60 seconds)
  • Rapid content consumption patterns
  • Different creator ecosystem than traditional YouTube
  • Intense competition with TikTok and Instagram
  • Need for non-disruptive ad formats

We needed to develop an advertising solution that would be effective for brands while maintaining the unique user experience that made Shorts successful.

Technical Implementation

Content Classification System

We built a specialized content classification system for short-form video:

  • Developed lightweight, low-latency classification models
  • Created short-form-specific content taxonomies
  • Built real-time processing pipeline for immediate classification
  • Implemented confidence scoring for all classifications

This system analyzed both the visual and audio components of Shorts content, classifying videos across multiple dimensions including topic, style, mood, and production quality.

Brand Safety & Suitability

We extended YouTube's existing brand safety frameworks for the unique challenges of short-form content:

  • Adapted traditional brand safety signals for short-form context
  • Developed new signals specific to Shorts content
  • Created tiered suitability levels for different brand requirements
  • Implemented continuous monitoring for policy violations

This framework ensured that brands could confidently advertise on Shorts without concerns about appearing alongside inappropriate content.

Ad Placement & Serving

We created new ad placement formats designed specifically for the Shorts viewing experience:

  • Non-disruptive overlay ads that preserved the viewing experience
  • Sponsored content indicators for creator collaborations
  • Interactive elements optimized for vertical video
  • Custom bidding and pricing models for short-form inventory

These formats were tested extensively with both users and advertisers to ensure they struck the right balance between effectiveness and user experience.

Cross-Team Coordination

A key aspect of this project was the extensive cross-functional coordination required. I initiated and led collaboration with multiple YouTube teams:

  • Shorts Product Team - Ensuring ad formats aligned with product vision
  • Creator Experience - Developing creator monetization opportunities
  • Ads Measurement - Building effectiveness metrics for short-form
  • Trust & Safety - Implementing appropriate content policies
  • Sales & Partner Teams - Educating advertisers on the new offering

This coordination was crucial for creating a holistic solution that addressed the needs of all stakeholders in the Shorts ecosystem.
